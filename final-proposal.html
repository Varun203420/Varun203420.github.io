Project Concept / Motivation

I want to build a handheld reflex-testing game that helps improve reaction time and focus. When one of the LEDs randomly lights up, the player must press the matching button as quickly as possible. The Arduino measures the delay between the LED turning on and the button press, then gives feedback through lights and sound. This could help gamers or athletes train their reflexes in a fun and portable way.
